In Account Information, you can complete your registration, update your contact information, change your account settings and manage your documents and shipping preferences. 

Complete Registration

If you’re not a Basic or Premier Member, here is where you’ll go to upgrade your Guest Membership and start bidding. Finish the final steps of becoming a Basic or Premier Member to get Buying Power, join multiple auctions at once, save your searches, and unlock other benefits.

Contact Information

Need to update your contact information? Change your phone number, email address and name in Contact Information. This section will also inform you how to change your physical address.

Account Settings

In Account Settings you can change the website language, update your password, manage your Membership, submit licenses (if you’re a business) and learn about selling with Copart.

Shipping & Documents

This section covers shipping, title delivery and other documents. Here is where you can update your preferences and select whether or not you want your vehicle title to be mailed to you.
